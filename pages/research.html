Research Hub

Kingsley Research Hub

Our Kingsley campus features a dedicated Research Hub located in Building A, with hot desks and standing desks in fully networked workstations. The hub also includes meeting rooms, collaborative pods, and spaces designed for innovation and research collaboration.

Innovative Staff

Kingsley Research Staff

Our Kingsley Research College (KRC) is a dedicated department designed to Enable Excellent Research and foster innovation.
The KRC achieves this by collaborating with key stakeholders, including postgraduate research students, faculty researchers, professional service teams across the university, and the host faculty overseeing your studies.

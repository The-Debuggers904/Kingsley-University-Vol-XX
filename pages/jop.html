application process

1

Resume & Cover Letter

A resume summarizes your skills and experience, while a cover letter highlights your enthusiasm and fit for the role. Together, they create a strong first impression and boost your chances of getting an interview.

2

Application Submission

Application submission is the process of providing required documents, like resumes and cover letters, to apply for a job or program. It’s your first step in showcasing your qualifications and interest.

3

Interview

An interview is a conversation between a candidate and an employer to assess qualifications, skills, and fit for a role. It’s a key step in the hiring process to showcase your strengths and personality.

4

Offer & Onboarding

An offer marks the employer’s decision to hire you, outlining terms of employment. Onboarding follows, helping you integrate into the company, understand your role, and start your journey successfully.
